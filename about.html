Company History

Where it all
began.

BeautyBontle M Copy Centre was registered with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) on the 20th of March 2018.

Reg No: 2018/212974/07

From day one, we committed to offering reliable internet and professional printing services to our local community. What started as a small neighbourhood copy centre has grown into a trusted destination for individuals and local businesses alike.

Located in Mapoteng, Sesheng in the Northern Cape, we proudly serve our community with a full suite of services — from prepaid WiFi and colour copies to large format signage and CV typing.

Business Goals & Objectives

Growing to serve
more than individuals.

Our goal is a simple one — to become a trusted provider in the space we operate in. To grow to a point where we can provide solutions even to businesses and not just individuals.

We believe the foundation of lasting growth lies in doing the basics exceptionally well: showing up, delivering quality, and making every customer feel valued every single time they walk through our doors.

Growth Strategy

Our growth will be driven by consistency — consistently meeting and even surpassing customer needs. The satisfaction our customers experience ensures they return and refer others. Smart marketing will further amplify our reach.

Director & Founder
Bontle Beauty
Monnagaaratwe

Born and raised in Batlharos, Kuruman, Bontle completed her Grade 12 at Lesedi High School in 2004. She went on to study a National Certificate in Microsoft Office Specialist and Comptia A+ at MSC Business College in Kempton Park from 2011 to 2013.

She also completed a Short Course in Construction and Project Management at Pro Ulwazi College in 2018. While studying, she began her career working part-time at a local internet café — and after completing her studies, she pursued her passion by founding her own business, serving the Mapoteng Kathu community in the Northern Cape.

"I believe in a homelike environment where every customer leaves satisfied. Our community deserves nothing less than excellent service."

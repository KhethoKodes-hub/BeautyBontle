Strategy & Vision

Driven by purpose,
guided by values.

Vision

To become a leading internet café in and around our area of operation. With a commitment to meet or even exceed our customer's needs. An internet café that is tagged as trusted and well liked.

Mission

To deliver excellent quality services. Operate in a responsible manner and provide a homelike environment. Bringing out a consistently satisfying customer experience.

Growth Strategy

Our growth will be driven by consistency — consistently meeting and even surpassing customer needs. Satisfaction ensures customers return and refer. Growing to serve businesses, not just individuals.

Our Values

What we stand for.

01
People

We care deeply about people. Our own people, partners, customers, and the community to which we belong. People are at the centre of everything we do.

02
Passion

Passion is the fuel that drives our culture. It defines our mindset and energises our business. We bring enthusiasm and dedication to every service we offer.

03
Place

We take seriously our responsibility to the environment. Through our actions we contribute towards creating a better place for future generations in our community.

Bontle Beauty
Monnagaaratwe

Born and raised in Batlharos, Kuruman, Bontle completed her Grade 12 at Lesedi High School in 2004 and went on to study a National Certificate in Microsoft Office Specialist and Comptia A+ at MSC Business College in Kempton Park (2011–2013). She also completed a Short Course in Construction and Project Management at Pro Ulwazi College in 2018.

After working part-time at a local internet café while studying, she pursued her passion and founded her own internet café serving the Mapoteng Kathu community in the Northern Cape.
